About

Hi! My name is Caroline Jarecke and in May 2019 I graduated with a degree in Computer Science with a Digital Media minor. I really enjoy incorporating art and coding together. According to the brain dominance theory, there are two sides of the brain: the left side - the logical side - and the right side - the creative side. While most people identify themselves as either 'left-brained' or 'right-brained,' I consider myself both-brained - I love the analytical, problem-solving aspects of computer science and the creativity and imagination of art!

I mostly have experience in front-end development, using HTML and CSS to make webpages look aesthetically pleasing. I also have a little bit of experience using JavaScript and jQuery, and recently I've started to learn React (I'm looking forward to learning more as well)! I've used Java and C# for various classes.

I can remember being 13 or 14 and using HTML to make my profile on a website look nice; that was my first experience with HTML, and I've been hooked since. I love it so much that I'd love to do it full-time. I'm very passionate about creating what I consider to be artwork with code. When I do something, I always go all-out in order to do the best that I can. I go into every day with a positive attitude, and I can't wait to put my creativity and passion into my work.

Thank you so much for taking the time to check out my website!
