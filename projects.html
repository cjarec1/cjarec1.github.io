Qwabble

Our team created a roommates app on Android and iOS that enabled roommates to view shared lists and calendars. I designed and implemented all of the images and UI for the app using XAML.

Friend Re-Quest

Our team created a multiplayer Android game in Unity for our Senior Capstone. Together, the team fights enemies and plays minigames where they have to cooperate to win. I created the user interface and the pixel backgrounds using GIMP. We had week-long sprints and weekly meetings.

Database Project

Our group created a database with a library, books, and users, and we used SQL queries to retrieve information.

Unreal Engine Final Project

Our team created a project in Unreal Engine where the player must go into different rooms and solve puzzles in order to escape. I created the level design and some simple assets for the game.

Unity Final Project

I created a simple game in Unity. The player is a roomba that needs to collect all the dust particles in the room while being chased by a cat NPC. I implemented most aspects of the game, including placing all of the objects in the room, editing the scripts so they fit my game, and worked with lighting and post-processing effects.

Project D.O.M.E.

Our team created a PC game in Unity where the player must survive for as long as possible. The player is attacked by enemies in the wild while trying to fortify the base. I created 3D enemies in Blender for this project.
